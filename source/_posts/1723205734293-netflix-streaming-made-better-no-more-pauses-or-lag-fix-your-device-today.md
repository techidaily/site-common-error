### Method 3: Troubleshoot your network connection issues

 If after trying method 1 & method 2, your Netflix still keeps freezing, it’s time to troubleshoot your network connection issues.

#### Way 1: Restart your network

1. Shut down your computer.
2. Unplug your modem and Wi-Fi router if you have one.
3. Wait for about 30 seconds and plug in your modem and router back.
4. Power on your computer and try Netflix again.
