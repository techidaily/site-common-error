## Solution 1: Replace the battery of your Surface pen

 The Surface pen requires a AAAA battery. There is one coming with your new Surface pen. Once you find i cannot work, like the buttons on it cannot function, when you use it writing, nothing shows up, the first thing you should do is trying to replace its battery with a new one. Then restart your Surface, and try to use your pen to see if it works.

## Solution 2: Repair the Surface pen with your tablet

 As we know, the Surface pen connect with the tablet via Bluetooth. If you find it not work anymore, try to repair it with your Surface tablet.

1) Use your finger to tap to open **Settings** on your Surface.

2) Tap on**Change PC settings**.  

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/3-16.png)

 3) On PC settings window, tap on**Bluetooth** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/4-15.png)

 Note: step 1) – 3) is to locate your Surface pen in Settings window. The steps shows in Windows 8.1\. For Windows 10 users, the steps are similar, just head to**Settings** —**Devices** —**Bluetooth and other devices** .

 4)Tap on your Surface pen and choose**Remove device** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/5-17.png)

 5) Then your Surface pen will be not shown under**Bluetooth** .  
 Press the**top button** on your pen for about 7 seconds until a green light flashing in the middle of your pen.
