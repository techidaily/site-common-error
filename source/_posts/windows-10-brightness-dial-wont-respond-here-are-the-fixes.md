No annoyance anymore! Here’s the guide to tell you how to fix the**brightness not working issue on Windows 10** . Read on and find out how. In general, your brightness control not working issue is due to the display driver. Here are two solutions you can try:

1. **[Update your display driver](#s1)**

3. Click **Browse my computer for driver software** .![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/img_595b183012265.png)
