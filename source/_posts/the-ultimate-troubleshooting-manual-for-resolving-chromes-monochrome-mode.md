### Fix 3: Check the connection

 You can only play COD: WW 2 with stable and constant internet. So check your connection from the console and try reconnecting to Xbox live. This bug could be related to the internet connection.

Use a wired connection if you’re a PC player.

* You need to make sure you’ve signed into your system account. If there’s a problem when signing into your account, it’s probably you have connection issues.

**Troubleshooting on** [**Xbox One**](https://beta.support.xbox.com/help/Hardware-Network/connect-network/network-settings#83dc7f0c62b1417fb8f85ef45393a1f1) **/** [**PS4**](https://support.playstation.com/s/article/Troubleshoot-Wired-or-Wireless-Network-Connection?language=en%5FUS) **/** [**Steam**](https://support.steampowered.com/kb%5Farticle.php?ref=1456-EUDN-2493) **.**

* If you were signed in but couldn’t connect, you need to check the connectivity alerts on your system’s status page.  
 PS4/Xbox one players are suggested to test your connection to find the culprit.

**Test connection** **For PS4 player**

1. From the PlayStation home screen, select **Settings** .
2. Select **Network** .
3. Select **Test Internet Connection** and allow the test to run.

**Test connection** **For Xbox one player**

1. Turn on your Xbox One without a disc inside and go to **Xbox Home** .
2. Go to **Settings** and select **Network** .
3. In the right-hand column, select **Test network connection** , and allow the test to run.

### Fix 4: Reinstall the game

 Uninstall the game and reinstall it could simply rewrite data files. The error code may be fixed by this fix.

1. Press**the Windows logo key and R** on your keyboard at the same time to invoke the Run box.
2. Type **appwiz.cpl** , then press **Enter** on your keyboard.  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/image-426.png)
3. Delete Call of Duty: World War II (right-click this game in the list and select **Uninstall** ).
4. Download the game from where you purchased it.
5. Try playing CoD: WWII and see if this fixes your black screen issue.
