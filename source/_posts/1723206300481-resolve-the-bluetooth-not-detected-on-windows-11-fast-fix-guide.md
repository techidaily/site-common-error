## Solution 3: Reinstall your Bluetooth driver

 2)Then click **Device Manager** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/2-10.png)

 3) Right-click on your Bluetooth driver to choose **Uninstall device** .
