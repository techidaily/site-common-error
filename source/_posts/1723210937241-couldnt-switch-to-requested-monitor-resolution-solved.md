## Fix 3: Flush the DNS cache

 If clearing the cache and cookies doesn’t fix the issue, clearing your DNS may help.

To do this, simply follow the instructions below:

 1) Press**Windows logo key + R** together on your keyboard. Type**cmd** and press**Shift + Ctrl + Enter** at the same time.

![open command prompt
](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/open-command-prompt.png)

 2) When the window**Do you want to allow this app to make changes to your device** appears, click**Yes** .

 3) Type the command**ipconfig /flushdns** and hit**Enter** .

![flush the DNS cache](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/flush-DNS-cache.png)
